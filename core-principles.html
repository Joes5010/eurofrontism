Core Principles

Here are the core principles of Eurofrontism:

  • Nationalism and Continentalism: A deep love and loyalty for one's own nation, coupled with a strong sense of shared European identity and destiny. This involves prioritizing the well-being and interests of European nations and peoples.
  • Independent European Nations: Belief in a Europe composed of sovereign and culturally independent nations, each maintaining its unique identity and self-governance.
  • Inter-European Cooperation against External Threats: Recognizing the need for European nations to stand together and cooperate against common enemies and external pressures, while preserving their individual sovereignty.
  • Cultural Independence: Emphasis on preserving and promoting the distinct cultures, traditions, and languages of European nations, resisting cultural homogenization.
  • Socially-Oriented Governance: Support for government policies that prioritize the well-being of the people, incorporating socialist ideas in terms of social safety nets and ensuring the general welfare of the citizenry.
  • Strong and Respected Government: Advocacy for a capable and respected government that can maintain order, enforce laws, and command the respect of all citizens, including the youth through fostering discipline.
  • Controlled and Legal Migration: Acceptance of legal immigration processes while opposing mass and illegal immigration, with a focus on maintaining social cohesion and national identity.
  • Rejection of Extremism: Opposition to all forms of political extremism, recognizing the importance of balance and reasoned discourse.
