Flag & Symbolism

The Eurofrontism flag represents the unity, progress, and shared identity of Europe. The design features:

  • A circle of gold stars on a deep blue background, symbolizing the unity and solidarity of the European people.
  • The stars are arranged in a spiral, representing the dynamic progress and forward momentum of the European project.
  • The blue color represents peace, stability, and the vastness of the European sky.
  • The gold color of the stars signifies the richness of European culture, heritage, and the value of its citizens.

The flag serves as a powerful emblem of our shared European identity and our commitment to a united and prosperous future.
