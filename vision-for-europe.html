Vision for Europe

Our vision for Europe is of a continent that is:

This includes:

  • A Europe of Strong, Independent Nations: We envision a Europe where individual nations retain their sovereignty, unique cultures, and traditions, fostering a rich tapestry of diverse identities.
  • United in Common Purpose: These independent nations would cooperate closely on matters of shared interest, such as trade, security, and addressing common challenges, forging a powerful and influential bloc on the world stage.
  • A Guardian of European Values: Europe would stand as a beacon of its core values: democracy, individual liberty, the rule of law, and a commitment to social justice.
  • A Prosperous and Innovative Continent: We strive for a Europe that leads the world in innovation, economic growth, and technological advancement, creating opportunities for all its citizens.
  • A Secure and Stable Homeland: Ensuring the safety and security of all Europeans from both internal and external threats is paramount, with strong borders and effective cooperation in defense and law enforcement.
